About Us

I have been in the grooming industry since I was 15 years old with my first job. I started out at a bather for 6 years and then taught by Vickie Piva at Blue Dog Pet Grooming. I had the honor of working for her for 5 years. Where I eventually became shop manager. I chose this profession because of my love for every breed of dog. Spending my day around these wonderful animals fulfills my childhood dream and fills me with so much joy.

Kippers Klippers is named after the Apple of my eye. My Pembroke Welsh corgi Kipper. With such a big heart he never fails to put a smile on anyone's face.
